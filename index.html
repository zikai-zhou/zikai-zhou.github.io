About

I am a Computer Science PhD student at Stanford University, advised by Prof. Monica S. Lam. I am affiliated with the Stanford Open Virtual Assistant Lab and the Stanford NLP Group. My research combines formal methods with large language models to tackle domains with complex structure, such as healthcare and job search. I build end-to-end systems that are deployed in real-world settings, with a focus on reliability and precision. Previously, I received my undergraduate degree from the University of Chicago.
